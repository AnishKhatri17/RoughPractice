"Daily Drinking Water For A Healthy Life"

Proper hydration is essential for maintaining a healthy body. The amount of water you need depends on your age, activity level, and environment and the climate you live in.

Recommended Daily Water Intake For A Healthy Life

"The human body can last weeks without food, but only days without water."

1-3 Years (Children)

1.3 - 2.1 liters (per day)

4-8 Years (Children)

1.7 - 2.4 liters (per day)

9-13 Years (Adolescents)

2.4 - 3.3 liters (per day)

14-18 Years (Teen Boys)

3.3 - 3.7 liters (per day)

14-18 Years (Teen Girls)

2.3 - 2.7 liters (per day)

Adults (Men)

3.7 liters (per day)

Adults (Women)

2.7 liters (per day)

NOTE : The above data are collected from reliable sources from the web. The daily water intake can vary depending upon the weather you live in, your lifestyle, how active you are, etc. For example, a pregnant or breastfeeding woman will require more amount of water intake daily than any normal adult woman.

Drink water at these key times: after waking up, before meals, during exercise, and before bed.


Best Time to Drink Water and Why?

1. After Waking Up

* Recommendation: Drink 1 glass (200-300ml).

* Reason: The body becomes slightly dehydrated during the sleep, and drinking water upon waking helps jumpstart metabolism and rehydrate tissues.

2. 30 Minutes Before Meals

* Recommendation: Drink 1 glass (200ml) '30 minutes before a meal'.

* Reason: Drinking water before eating can aid digestion by preparing the stomach lining and improving nutrient absorption.

3. During Meals

* Recommendation: Take small sips as needed (About 100ml Total) .

* Reason: Sipping water during meals can aid the swallowing process and prevent dehydration. However, don't drink excessive water during meals.

4. Mid-Morning and Mid-Afternoon

* Recommendation: Drink 1 glass (200-300ml) during each break.

* Reason: These are common times for energy dips. Drinking water can help maintain focus and prevent fatigue.

5. Before Physical Activity

* Recommendation: Drink 1-2 glasses (250-500ml) about 30 minutes before exercise.

* Reason: Pre-hydrating ensures that your body is adequately prepared to handle sweat lose and maintain performance levels.

6. During Physical Activity

* Recommendation: Sip 100-200ml every 20 minutes depending on sweat rate anad intensity.

* Reason: Water maintains hydration, regulates body temperature and prevents fatigue.

7. After Physical Activity

* Recommendation: Drink atleast 500ml.

* Reason: Re-supplies fluids lost through sweat and supports recovery.

8. In the Afternoon (Post-Lunch)

* Recommendation: Drink 1 glass (200-300ml).

* Reason: Drinking water mid-afternoon helps and prevents dehydration during long work or study hours.

9. Before Bed

* Recommendation: Drink 1 small glass (100-200ml).

* Reason: Hydration before bed prevents dehydration during sleep. However, avoid drinking large amounts to prevent noctural urination.
